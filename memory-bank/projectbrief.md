# ClipForge Project Brief

## Project Overview

ClipForge is a modern, lightweight desktop video editor designed for the core editing workflow: **Import ‚Üí Preview ‚Üí Trim ‚Üí Export**. Built with Electron and React, it delivers professional-grade video editing capabilities with zero external dependencies and 100% offline functionality.

## Core Value Proposition

A fast, intuitive video trimming tool that combines the power of professional video editing with the simplicity of modern desktop applications, perfect for creators who need to quickly cut and export video clips without the complexity of enterprise editing software.

## Target Users

- Content creators
- Video editors
- Professionals who need quick video trimming
- Users who want offline video editing capabilities
- Anyone needing simple, reliable video editing
